About IntelKHAT
IntelKHAT is an AI-powered system developed to recognize Arabic calligraphy styles from uploaded images.
Project Purpose
To preserve Islamic artistic heritage and help students, beginners, and researchers identify khat styles more easily.
Target Users
Students, KAFA learners, beginners in khat, educators, and Islamic art enthusiasts.
How to Use
1 → Upload an image of Arabic calligraphy
2 → Click “Detect Khat”
3 → Wait for AI prediction
4 → View the top detected khat style
Suitable Uses
• KAFA and school learning
• Islamic art appreciation
• Beginner khat reference
• Digital heritage preservation
